English Adjective Game

Adjective Forms:

• Positive: The base form of the adjective (e.g., "happy").

• Comparative: Used to compare two things. Add "-er" or use "more" (e.g., "happier" or "more beautiful").

• Superlative: Used to compare three or more things. Add "-est" or use "most" (e.g., "happiest" or "most beautiful").
